\# RL Summary



The RL controller was implemented as an advanced extension to the project.



\## Compared systems

\- baseline medium

\- original RL medium

\- improved RL medium



\## Outcome

The fixed-time baseline remained the strongest performer on the medium scenario. The improved RL controller functioned correctly but did not outperform the baseline.



\## Interpretation

This result suggests that while RL can be integrated successfully, its effectiveness depends heavily on state representation, reward design, and training complexity. For this project, RL is best presented as an advanced experimental extension rather than the strongest-performing controller.
