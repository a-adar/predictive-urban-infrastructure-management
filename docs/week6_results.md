\## Week 6 Incident Handling Results



An incident-style traffic surge scenario was introduced by increasing traffic demand relative to the normal medium scenario.



Two systems were tested:

\- fixed-time baseline

\- AI-driven adaptive controller



Metrics collected:

\- vehicles completed

\- average duration

\- average waiting time

\- average time loss



This experiment evaluates the robustness of the controller under dynamic, high-demand conditions and supports the project requirement for stress testing and incident recovery analysis.
