\# Week 1 Notes



\## Project Title

AI-Driven Predictive Traffic Signal Management for Urban Infrastructure



\## Project Summary

This project builds a simulated urban traffic network where an AI model predicts short-term congestion and dynamically adjusts traffic signals to improve traffic flow compared with a fixed-time baseline.



\## Chosen Focus

Traffic management (not full urban infrastructure).



\## System Components

1\. Traffic simulation (SUMO)

2\. Data collection (traffic metrics)

3\. Forecasting model (predict congestion)

4\. Adaptive controller (traffic light timing)

5\. Dashboard (visualisation)



\## Simulation Scope

\- 4 intersections

\- 2 main roads

\- 2 side roads



\## Traffic Scenarios

1\. Normal traffic

2\. Rush hour (high demand)

3\. Incident (road closure or surge)



\## Baseline System

Fixed-time traffic lights with no adaptation.



\## AI Approach

\- Predict queue length in next time step

\- Adjust traffic light timing based on prediction



\## Evaluation Metrics

\- Average waiting time

\- Queue length

\- Throughput

\- Forecast MAE / RMSE

\- Recovery time after disruption
